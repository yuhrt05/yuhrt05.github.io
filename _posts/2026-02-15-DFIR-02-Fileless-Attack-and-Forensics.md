## Description


> It started as a routine system optimization. A Finance employee executed a tool promising a faster PC, but instead, it delivered a nightmare. Within minutes, the LabData folder was encrypted, and a ransom demand appeared. Forensic investigators suspect a Fileless Ransomware attack—a ghost in the machine that leaves no trace on the disk. A memory dump was captured while the threat was still active.
> Your Mission: Analyze the memory dump, find the attacker's traces, and answer the following questions!
